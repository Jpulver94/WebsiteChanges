Options for Orders on Hold
The following orders are on hold:
  1. Click on "Resume" to pick up and continue an older order. Resuming an order will put your current cart on hold.
  2. Click on "Combine" to add the items in your cart to an existing order on hold. Note that when an existing order is combined with items in your shopping cart, all of the existing order's settings will become primary and the ship-to address, customer po, etc. will be updated with the information from the already existing order. To update the Shipping Information, Customer PO, etc., please visit the "Shipping Information" option under "Place an Order" after the orders are combined.
  3. To Delete orders from this list, click to "Resume" the order and then click the "Delete Order" button at the bottom of the cart page. Follow the instructions on the page to confirm the order's deletion. Note: Once an order is deleted, it cannot be recovered or resumed.
